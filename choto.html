Astrophysics in the Eyes of Love

By: The Protagonist, A Science Student

As a student of science, I have always been fascinated by the universe’s laws — the way they govern everything, from the smallest particles to the vast expanse of space. But what if I told you that these laws also apply to something as intangible as love? Let me explain.

Nature's Laws and Emotional Gravity

I liken you to the shore and me to the waves of the sea. Just as every wave is drawn to the shore due to the unbreakable forces of nature, my feelings for you are inevitable. Like the laws of physics, I feel a pull towards you that I cannot resist. It's a force greater than myself, constant and powerful. The pull of your presence in my life is as undeniable as gravity itself.

A Heart Turning Red

When you're near me, my heart turns red—alive with emotion, vulnerable to your touch. Without you, I feel like an orphaned child, crying for a love that's out of reach. My right brain imagines a future where our story begins, where you come to me and hold my hand. It's a longing so deep, so intense, yet filled with hope.

Color, Light, and Darkness

Darkness fills my world, but you, you are the light that could turn it all into color. You’re the light that softens my blackness, that fades the darkness until it turns to white. Without your presence, I feel incomplete, like a painting that has yet to see its full color.

The Black Hole of Emotion

My love for you is like a black hole—a force of nature that no one fully understands. It's mysterious and consuming, pulling me in despite all logic. Like a black hole, it draws everything around it, and though no one can explain it, its presence is undeniable. I can't help but feel this way about you.

Waves on the Shore of the Heart

I want to be the wave that comes to your shore, taking away your pain and casting it into the unknown. Just as waves shape the shore, I hope to shape your life, bringing relief and comfort where it’s needed most. My presence in your life would be like the constant ebb and flow of the sea — always there, always with you.

Double-Slit Experiment: Love as Transformation

In a moment, like in the double-slit experiment, I will change myself when I see you. Just as a particle behaves differently when observed, I will adjust my very nature, embodying whatever you need, becoming the lover of your dreams. It’s a strange thing, this love — so adaptable, so responsive.

Orbit and Gravity

Your presence is like the Sun, and I, like a planet, am drawn to you. I orbit you, forever spinning around the center of your gravitational pull. You are the essence that keeps me grounded and the force that keeps me moving forward. Without you, I would be adrift in space, lost without direction.

Electron and External Forces

You are the external force that has energized me to reach new heights. Just as an electron is pushed to a higher energy state by outside forces, you have given me the strength to pursue my dreams. I was once stagnant, but you pushed me to grow, to strive for something greater.

Admiration and Vulnerability

My love for you goes beyond physical attraction. I admire your strength, your bravery, and your spirit. You are a courageous soul, and I see in you a light that has helped me find my own. I don’t know where life will take us, but I will wait. I will wait for you, no matter what, because my love for you will never fade.

A Final Plea

"If my love has its highest form, you will come. And if not, know this: my love for you will never decrease. You can call on me at any point in your life, and I will definitely come."

You are the force that has shaped me, the light that illuminates my darkest moments. No matter where you go or who you are with, my admiration and love for you will remain constant. You are my universe.
