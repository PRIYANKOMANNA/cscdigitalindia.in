About Us

The best G2C B2B B2C G2B solution with 10 years of experience

Common Service Centers (CSCs) in India offer a variety of services to citizens, especially in rural and remote areas. Some of the services commonly provided by CSCs include:

1. Government to Citizen (G2C) Services: - Aadhaar Enrollment and Updates - PAN Card Services - Passport Application Services - Voter ID Card Services - Birth and Death Certificate Services 2. Financial Services: - Banking Services - Micro-Insurance Services - Pension Services 3. Utility Bill Payments: - Electricity Bill Payments - Water Bill Payments - Gas Bill Payments 4. Telecom Services: - Mobile Recharge and Bill Payments - DTH Recharge 5. Internet and Digital Services: - Internet Browsing - Digital Literacy Training - Digital Payment Services 6. Educational Services: - Online Course Enrollment - Exam Form Submission - Certificate Verification 7. Healthcare Services: - Telemedicine Consultation - Health Insurance Enrollment 8. Rural Development Services: - Employment Registration - Agriculture Services - Rural BPO Services 9. E-Governance Services: - Application Forms for Government Schemes - Grievance Redressal These services aim to make various government and essential services accessible to citizens, especially those in rural and remote areas.
